About this Product

This product is used to help individuals track their emotional, physical, and mental health. It lets people create and sign in to their own account for free. It also lets them track how they were, and how they felt the day was at the end of the day. Individuals are asked a list of guiding questions that helps track their health, as well as a final question where they can add an facts or comments that they want to add about the day. Users are also able to look at pervious days answers, and are shown a chart of the past fourteen days, and how they overall were shown as emojis. If you want to use this product, click the sign in button at the top of the page to create and account and start your online diary today.
